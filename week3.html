Why Accessibility is important?

There are 60 million with a disability in the U.S. & half of them are impeded from using the internet. The issues vary from visual issues, hearing issues, motor issues & cognitive issues.

What is web accessibility?

In simple terms, web accessibility is making the web accessible for the widest range possible. This audience includes Temporarily Able-Bodied users (TABs). Currently, online infrastructure is hostile to those with disabilities. Inseparable from SEO, mobile, and usability: improve one and you improve the others.

The best way to make the web accessible to everyone is adherence to standards.
